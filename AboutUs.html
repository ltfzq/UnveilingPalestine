About Us

Our group, comprised of passionate Computer Science Mobile Computing students from Universiti Malaysia Terengganu, is driven by a shared commitment to spreading awareness about the ongoing conflict in Palestine. Motivated by a collective desire to unveil the truth behind the complexities of the situation, we aim to shed light on the untold stories and provide a platform for open dialogue. Through our dedicated efforts, we strive to bring attention to the human stories, historical context, and current challenges faced by the people of Palestine. With a blend of technological skills and a deep sense of empathy, our mission is to leverage our expertise to contribute meaningfully to the discourse surrounding this critical global issue.
