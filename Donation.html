Donation Programs

The "Palestin Diserang" campaign is an Emergency Aid Fund to collect funds to be distributed to those in need in Palestine, whether during or after the war. This assistance is fully managed by the MyCARE Office in Gaza and the representatives of humanitarian NGOs in the West Bank.
MyFundAction’s is collaborating with UFUK Insani Yardin Energi, one NGO stay in Palestine and our team collaborators is on the ground, ready to provide vital assistance with your help. We Are Committed to Providing: medications and medical equipment for hospitals,fuel for ambulances and hospitals to power electric generators and urgent Food supply. Let’s stand united in solidarity with the people of Palestine. Your generosity can make a world of difference!
The context in the State of Palestine remains a protracted humanitarian crisis, where too many Palestinians struggle to live in dignity. MERCY Malaysia will continue to mobilize humanitarian assistance in order to help meet the basic needs of vulnerable Palestinians living under occupation.
May victory be on the side of truth and evil will continue to be punished! You can channel to emergency4gaza.com or the official account of the Love Gaza Malaysia Association, Maybank 5648 1050 7826. Ref: EMERGENCY.
