Mission and Vision

Mission

  • Utilize computer science and mobile computing skills for innovative platforms and campaigns.
  • Develop technology-driven solutions to effectively convey the realities of the Palestinian conflict.
  • Provide a comprehensive and unbiased perspective to reach a broad audience.

Vision

  • Foster a world of widespread awareness and understanding of the Palestinian conflict.
  • Cultivate empathy, informed discussions, and global efforts for a just and lasting resolution.
  • Create a space for diverse individuals to unite towards the common goal of peace, justice, and dignity in the region.
